Safety Checklist

Safety glasses

No loose fitting clothing

No open toe shoes or sandals

Users must clean up area used every time work is finished

Students must clean and return all tools

No horseplay allowed

Floor area where work was done must be swept after every use

Report any broken tools or machines immediately

All injuries (including minor) must be reported immediately

No hanging object should be over 10 feet above ground level

Clear pathways from obstacles

Follow pit schedule

Wash hands after handling with grease, dust, or any parts

Ensure that anything that potentially sparks is in a cool area

Reminders:

  • Safety glasses must be worn at all times in the pit area
  • Hearing protection is available – check the safety kit
  • No horseplay- Do not distract others, and please follow the schedule
  • Know your safety procedures for pit operation, if you’re not sure, don’t be afraid to ask
  • Keep tools in an orderly manner at all times
  • Practice safe lifting- make sure your back is straight
  • Follow the robot cart procedures (stable positioning, no unnecessary items, etc.)
  • Follow the dress code! No open toed shoes, shorts, or loose articles of clothing
  • Report all injuries to the Safety Captain or a Mentor
  • Review the Safety Rules provided
  • Show respect to all and have fun!

Queuing Checklist

Bumpers

Ensure they are the correct color

Pins are attached

Electrical

Update battery log

Put new battery in

Ensure that all motors and talons are connected

Make sure camera is plugged in

Make sure wires are not loose/slack

Make sure the radio is secured

Organize batteries according to date

Mechanical (general)

Chain tension fixed

Bolts and nuts secured

Mechanical (queuing)

All tools in proper places

Nothing unnecessary on the cart (nothing should be on the cart ever)

Cart is stable and robot is placed in a level and balanced position

All members of the driveteam are updated on the status of the robot and are ready to go

Robot has no loose parts (nuts, bolts, metal, etc)

Is the technician with you? And adding the bumpers to the robot?

Arrive maximum 5 minutes before the match starts

Do not do any sort of cutting/drilling work on top of a motor

Only driveteam and specific members (that are needed) should be in queue area

All remaining members of mechanical clean up to set up for next session in the pits

People in the queue area: make sure to check with the other subteams that everything is good to go (see the checklist)
